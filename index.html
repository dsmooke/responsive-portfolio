About Me

My name is Dana "Danny" Smooke. I am 25 years old, and I graduated from Yale University with a double major in Theater Studies and Art History. My first introduction to any kind of web desiGN was a summer course on Adobe InDesign and Photoshop. I loved how I was able to create professional looking layouts using my own style.

This the summer was my first experience working with any type of coding language. So when it became clear that all schools would need to transition to some form of online teaching platform, the need for developers skyrocketed and I wanted to help. Using LaTeX, I helped convert my father's handwritten technical notes into .png images to use in PowerPoint.

When I'm not in the theater or behind the computer, you can find me working outside in the yard, doing a crossword puzzle, working out, cooking, or drawing political cartoons. My website.
