Portfolio

  • Artwork

    • Mediums range from watercolor, charcoal, oil, pencil, acrylic, and ink. Landscapes, Georgia O'Keefe style. Simple subjects but high contrast.

  • Theater

    • She has been a costume designer, director, makeup artist, hair and wig stylist, and fight captain, among other positions, and has worked on up to sixteen shows during her time at Yale.

  • Costumes

    • As a double major in theater and art history, her primary focus is the overlap between fashion history and emotion. In other words, how a garment’s pattern, color, and texture can reveal a character’s emotions, personality, and subtext. Some of her costume trademarks include her use of bold colors and simplicity in design.
